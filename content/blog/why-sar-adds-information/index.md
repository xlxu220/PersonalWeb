Passive radiometry gives you brightness temperature at coarse resolution; SAR gives you backscatter at fine resolution but tangled with roughness and vegetation. The interesting question is not "which is better" but **what independent information each adds**, and how to combine them without double-counting. That tension is the heart of the SMAP–NISAR active/passive problem.
