- Soil Moisture Estimates Using L-Band Airborne SAR Over Forests Replicating NISAR Observations. *IEEE JSTARS* (2025).
- A Merged CYGNSS Soil Moisture Product Using a Minimum Variance Estimator. *IEEE TGRS* (2025).
- SMAP Validation Experiment 2019–2022 (SMAPVEX19-22): Field Campaign to Improve Soil Moisture and Vegetation Optical Depth Retrievals in Temperate Forests. *IEEE JSTARS* (2025).
