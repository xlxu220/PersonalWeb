This is a **new mission concept** still taking shape. By reaching to **lower frequencies**, it would sense **deeper into soil and vegetation** than the L-band I work with today — opening new terrain for the same wave-physics-first approach.

The science questions are familiar — water in the ground, structure in the canopy — but a new frequency means new scattering physics to model from first principles before any retrieval can be trusted. More as the concept matures.
