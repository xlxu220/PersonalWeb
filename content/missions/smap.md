NASA's **Soil Moisture Active Passive** mission has been the center of my career. I'm a lead developer of its **freeze–thaw** algorithm, work on the **L-band soil-moisture** retrieval, and carry that lineage forward into the joint **SMAP–NISAR** algorithm.

SMAP is where a piece of wave physics becomes a product the world relies on — measured at L-band, validated against the ground, and delivered season after season.
