CYGNSS reads soil moisture from a different kind of signal — the **navigation-satellite signals that reflect off the land surface** (GNSS-R). I built JPL's **Level-3 soil-moisture product** from CYGNSS, and extended it to **vegetation optical depth** from CYGNSS v3.

It's the same question as SMAP — how much water is in the ground — approached through a completely different measurement, which makes the underlying physics all the more important to get right.
